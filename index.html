WELCOME LSP Hotel

Welcome to LSP Hotel, where every stay is a journey of discovery and delight. Nestled in the heart of Sihanoukville, Cambodia’s vibrant coastal city, our hotel combines artistic design, modern comfort, and unparalleled service to redefine your travel experience.

LSP HOTEL

LSP Hotel provides accommodations in Sihanoukville near Wat Leu and Sihanoukville Bus Terminal. With a shared lounge, the 4-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The property is non-smoking throughout and is located 2.5 miles from Serendipity Beach Pier. Kbal Chhay Waterfalls is 8 miles from the accommodation, while Soriya Bus Station Sihanoukville is 1.5 miles from the property. Sihanouk International Airport is 10 miles away.

ROOMS

Each room is thoughtfully designed with calm lighting, soothing tones, and a harmonious balance of textures. The interiors are adorned with artistic wall features and high-quality wooden accents that create a serene ambiance, perfect for both business and leisure travelers. All 75 rooms are comfortably appointed and well maintained giving it an air of tranquillity.
