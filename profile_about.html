LSP Hotel – Your Tranquil Retreat

Thank you for visiting the website of pich lisa hotel

Nestled in the heart of Sihanoukville, LSP Hotel offers an exceptional blend of modern comfort and serene aesthetics

Conveniently located near Wat Leu and the Sihanoukville Bus Terminal, our 4-star property provides easy access to the city's key attractions, including Serendipity Beach Pier (2.5 miles) and the picturesque Kbal Chhay Waterfalls (8 miles).

Our 75 cozy and stylish rooms are designed with a simple and calm style, featuring soft lighting and natural wooden touches. Each room includes air-conditioning, free WiFi, a private bathroom, an electric tea pot, and a refrigerator for your comfort. Some rooms also have private balconies with beautiful city views and sea view, making your stay at LSP Hotel peaceful and enjoyable.

At LSP Hotel, our 24-hour front desk team is always ready to assist, communicating fluently in English, Khmer, and Chinese to ensure seamless communication and personalized service throughout your stay.
